OUR STORY

Hope Church Lavington was started in 2002 as an Orlando Baptist Church plant by Dr. Dave Janney. We are located at the Hope Center along Serenity Road off Amboseli Road. The Church was started with the main aim of reaching the people of Nairobi Kenya with the gospel of Christ. We are a non-denominational Church. Our efforts have seen us partner with various organizations namely Vapor Sports Ministries, AWANA, Zion Church in the U.S.A and One Child Matters. We currently have a single service running from 10.30am to 12.30pm every Sunday.

Our membership has steadily grown to 300 members with the children's church having at least 400 kids attending every Sunday. We have a growing life-group ministry taking place on various days of the week at homes and within the Church compound. We also have different group meetings on Sunday after themain service and our Bible Clubs for kids and teens running every Saturday afternoon from 2pm to 4pm.
